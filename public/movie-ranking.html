My Top 3 animes of all-times:

Shingeki No Kyojin

Attack on Titan" is known for its intense action scenes, complex characters, and deep themes, including the nature of freedom, the horrors of war, and the ambiguity of morality. The series has been praised for its storytelling, character development, and unique setting. It has also sparked discussions on topics like nationalism, imperialism, and the cycle of hatred.

Naruto

Naruto is one of the best-selling manga series of all time and has left a lasting impact on the anime and manga industry. Its influence extends to popular culture, inspiring a new generation of anime fans and creators. The series has been praised for its character development, world-building, and compelling storylines.

Kimentsu No Yaiba

The anime adaptation of "Demon Slayer," produced by Ufotable, debuted in 2019 and quickly became a global phenomenon. The series is praised for its stunning animation, particularly during fight scenes, and its faithful adaptation of the manga. The animation studio's use of vibrant colors and fluid movement has set a new standard for anime visuals. One of the most significant moments in the anime's success was the release of "Demon Slayer: Mugen Train," a feature film that directly continues the story from the anime's first season. The film became the highest-grossing film in Japanese history and one of the highest-grossing animated films worldwide.
